Impressionism

Computational Impressionism

After reading Matt's article on generating impressionist style artwork by using perlin noise flows, I was curious and decided to try the technique myself. The technique itself is quite simple, and involves mapping every pixel of a generated perlin noise field to an angle, which is then used to calculate the velocities of a particle system.
